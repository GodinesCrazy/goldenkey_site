Voxtide - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-29

Voxtide is a podcast player developed by Golden Key Studios. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1) Information We Collect

  • No names, emails, or account data are collected by the app.
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK and Google UMP (consent) may process device identifiers and ad signals subject to your choices.
  • The app requests podcast/media metadata from external providers (e.g., RSS/iTunes/YouTube endpoints).

2) Local Data & Storage

  • Preferences are stored locally via Android DataStore.
  • Downloaded audio is stored on your device; manage it in Library > Downloads.

3) Third-Party Services

  • Advertising: Google AdMob/UMP. See Google policies for details on data processing and consent.
  • Billing: Google Play Billing processes purchases; we do not receive payment card details.
  • Networking: OkHttp fetches content; providers receive your IP and standard headers.

4) Cookies/Tracking

The app does not set cookies. Third-party SDKs may use identifiers for ads/analytics per their policies and your consent.

5) Children's Privacy

Voxtide is not directed to children under 13. Do not use where prohibited by local laws.

6) Security

We follow platform best practices, but no method is 100% secure. Protect your device to safeguard downloads.

7) Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated in-app or via our site.
